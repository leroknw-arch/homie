# HOMIE Demo Guide

## Start Here

1. Log in with `ariana@homie.app` / `demo1234`.
2. Open `/dashboard` first to tell the executive story: budget, ROI, risk and workload in one screen.
3. Use global filters at the top to show how fast HOMIE narrows the portfolio.

## Best Campaigns To Demo

- `Aurora Pulse Launch`
  Shows campaign risk, blocked dependencies, lagging progress and a more complex cross-functional setup.
- `Luma Summer Glow`
  Shows a healthy, high-ROI execution flow with strong progress and clear team coordination.
- `Q2 Demand Engine`
  Shows a high-budget, high-priority campaign with clear budget pacing and sales alignment.

## Main Value Story

- HOMIE is not a generic project manager.
- It connects company, product, campaign, unit of work, deliverable and task structure into one executive system.
- Leaders can read budget, ROI, risk, capacity and upcoming deadlines quickly, then drill into execution only when needed.

## Suggested Click Path

1. Dashboard
2. Apply a company or status filter
3. Open `Aurora Pulse Launch`
4. Move through the campaign tabs
5. Open `Gantt`
6. Open `Teams`

## What To Highlight

- Global filters affect every executive view consistently.
- Risk is derived from real execution conditions, not a manual flag.
- Budget, spend and ROI stay visible from overview to detail.
- Team workload is readable by both function and person.
